About Us:

Join three best friends as they discuss stories involving true crime, mystery and the supernatural. No topic is off the table for these ladies! With three unique perspectives, you're sure to find what you're looking for whether it's fright, knowledge or just a good laugh. Violent Delights invites you to listen, offer your own perspective and even submit your own topics for future episodes!
